question : what
will the cnerps certification exam be like to take ?
answer :
the cnerps certification will be all multiple choice with one single
right answer . unlike cierp you will not have to choose multiple
items from a list to get the right answer . a student will be able to
take the exam online at home for a low cost . ( expected to be $ 20 to
$ 40 usd ) about 100 to 125 question are expected . the online test
system will allow the user to review the status of all questions and
jump around in the test . once the test is completed the system will
automatically grade it providing for nearly instant test results .
