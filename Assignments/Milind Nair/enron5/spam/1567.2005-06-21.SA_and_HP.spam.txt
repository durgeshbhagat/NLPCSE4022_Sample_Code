providing environmental solutions through advanced technology
megola inc . is a publicly traded company operating in the multi - billion dollar water treatment industry , serving a wide variety of residential , commercial , industrial and agricultural clients . the company serves its clients by supplying products and tailor - made solutions to solve physical water treatment , microbiological control , wastewater treatment and air purification problems . megola ' s solutions enable companies to save money and boost productivity . the world is becoming more environmentally conscious . this is yet another trend that could benefit megola ' s business , as megola ' s physical water treatment solutions do not require the use of salt or harmful chemicals .
business model
megola ' s model is based on forming powerful alliances with its clients . instead of merely selling water treatment products , megola wants to position itself as the leading total service provider in its industry . megola teams up with its clients and becomes their business partner by assuming total responsibility for solving their water or air related problems
megola ' s goal is to minimize its clients ' costs and maximize their productivity and profitability . in the past few years , megola has learned a lot about the specific problems various industries are encountering with water and air quality . megola has learned how to solve those problems and by doing so , saved its clients hundreds of thousands of dollars .
market potential
the u . s . corrosion and scale - inhibitors industry , combined with the softener industry , is worth $ 737 million , and estimated to grow to $ 775 million by 2007 . the u . s . water treatment chemical market is projected to grow at an average annual rate of 4 . 1 % , reaching more than $ 2 . 7 billion by 2007 .
hard water is present in approximately 80 % of north american households . due to environmental issues , a growing number of communities in north america are banning the use of traditional salt softeners . this creates a powerful market opportunity for megola and its revolutionary , cost - effective and environmentally - friendly scaleguard system . areas such as southern california and texas , where water quality issues abound , are potential hotspots of market activity for environmental solutions to hard water problems .
